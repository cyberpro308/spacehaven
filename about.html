How it began

A small kitchen with a big dream

It started with Chef Funke cooking weekend meals for friends in a tiny Surulere kitchen. Word spread fast — the jollof was too good to keep secret. In 2013 we opened our doors on Victoria Island, and we've been feeding Lagos ever since.

Today, Space Haven is a beloved neighbourhood spot, but our promise hasn't changed: honest, generous Nigerian food made from scratch, every single day.
